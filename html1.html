No, I'm not one of those fashionistas that believe in dividing the shoppers between two categories: the winter ones, and the summer ones. Simply put, within every season, we wear the ones which compliment our current fashion style, or are a fun addition to it.

My personal obsession with this fashion piece is so huge, that I would break the unwritten rules of weather seasons any time. I am a personal believer in the fact that it is impossible to have enough shoppers in your closet.

Every season, on the popular brand-store shelves new shopper models appear, which preocupy my attention and spark a desire to go and "clean" my closet-space, in order to make room for those brand-new models. This tradition continues this fall as well, so in the tireless search for the prettiest shopper models, I have decided to sniff through the websites of everyone's favourite brands.

I made a selection of 3 fabulous purses for you and only you, which will become the new favourite member of your closet. On my list there are several leather shoppers, in multiple colors (among which there is a classic black, cognac and brown, typical fall colors you could say), and the ones decorated with super-popular snake-like and pearly patterns!
